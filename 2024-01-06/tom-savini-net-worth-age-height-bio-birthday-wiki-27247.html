    Top Facts about Tom Savini

  • Tom Savini is an American actor, stuntman, director and makeup artist.
  • He was born on November 3, 1946 in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania.
  • Savini served as a combat photographer during the Vietnam War.
  • He has worked on over 100 films including “Dawn of the Dead” and “Friday the 13th”.
  • In addition to his film work, he has also directed episodes of TV shows like “Tales from the Darkside”.
  • Savini won a Saturn Award for Best Makeup for his work on “Night of the Living Dead” (1990).
  • He has also written several books about special effects makeup.
  • Savini was inducted into the Horror Hall of Fame in 2006.
  • He retired from acting in 2010 but continues to teach at his special effects school.
  • His signature look includes a handlebar mustache and earrings in both ears.
