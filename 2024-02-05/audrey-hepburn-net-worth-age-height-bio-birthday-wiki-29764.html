    Audrey Hepburn Biography

    Audrey Hepburn is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actress who was born on May 4, 1929 in Ixelles, Belgium. Iconic star of Breakfast at Tiffany’s who won an Oscar, a BAFTA Award, and a Golden Globe Award for her performance in Roman Holiday. In 1993, she received a Screen Actors Guild Life Achievement Award.

    She co-starred with Gregory Peck in the 1953 romantic comedy Roman Holiday.

    She married Mel Ferrer in 1954, and the couple divorced in 1968. She had two sons named Sean Hepburn and Luca Dotti.
