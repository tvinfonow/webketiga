Timothy Gregg Bachman (born August 1st 1951) is a Canadian musician and vocalist who is famous for his work in rock groups Brave Belt and Bachman-Turner Overdrive (BTO). Bachman was among the original four members in BTO which has sold more than 30 million albums around the world. The group also included the brothers Randy (guitar/vocals) as well as Robbie (drums) and along with Fred Turner (bass/vocals).
