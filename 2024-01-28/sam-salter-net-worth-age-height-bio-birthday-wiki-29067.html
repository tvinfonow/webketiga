    Sam Salter Biography

    Sam Salter is one of the most popular and richest Singer-Songwriter who was born on February 16, 1975 in Los Angeles, California, United States. Samuel Salter (born February 16 1975) is a Pop, Dance, EDM, R&B, Soul, Rap and Country music producer and a writer. He was raised singing gospel music in the Faithful Church of God in Christ in Los Angeles. As a child, Salter won several local talent shows. He had a voice that was inspired from Marvin Gaye and Stevie Wonder.

    In 2010 he released the single “Straight 2 Heaven” independently. Later, he joined Elemental Entertainment INC, and released two singles on digital formats (10’s “Got Me” and 11’s “To Be Loved”)

    In early 2016, Salter signed with artist management company Flight Club Entertainment.

    As of 2005 Salter was featured on a CD single titled “On A September Morn” as part of a group known as Talking Book. The following year, he started work to record his third project called”Strictly for Da Bedroom that was scheduled for release in 2008. In addition, he collaborated together with Frankie J on a couple of tracks on his album Time (which later was renamed Courage).
