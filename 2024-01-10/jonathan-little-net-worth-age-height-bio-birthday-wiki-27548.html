    Jonathan Little Biography

    Jonathan Little is one of the most popular and richest Poker Player who was born on December 22, 1984 in Pensacola, Florida, United States. Jonathan Little (born December 22 1984, in Pensacola, Florida) is an American professional poker player who has won WPT’s Season VI Mirage Poker Showdown as well as Season VII Foxwoods World Poker Finals and was awarded the WPT Season VI Player of the Year award.

    In the 2009 World Championship of Online Poker in 2008, with his PokerStars screen under the name “Jcardshark”, Little finished 7th in the buy-in of $10,000 (event #5) and earned $107,535 from an end-of-day table that included others well-known players such as Major League Baseball Pitcher Orel Hershiser (O. Hershiser) the 2003 World Series of Poker Main Event Champion Chris Moneymaker (Money800) online pros Nick Niergarth (gbmantis), Greg Hobson (DuckU), Scott Dorin (dorinvandy) who took home the event , as well as the European Poker Tour Season IV Grand Final Champion Glen Chorny (Choron). The victory brings Little’s online total of cash wins on Pokerstars to more than $280,000.

    Little married on August 1, 2015 to Amie Broder. His best man was fellow poker player, Shannon Shorr. When Little is not traveling the live poker circuit, he teaches his students how to improve their poker strategies at PokerCoaching.com.

    As of February 2019, his total live tournament winnings exceed $6,800,000.

    The month of March was when Full Tilt Poker announced that it had removed Little from its roster of professional sponsored by the company because he had violated the terms and conditions of its service by allowing other players to play on his Full Tilt account. Through His blog Little apologized for the actions he took, and issued an apology Full Tilt.
