    Chris Santos is one of the most popular and richest Chef who was born on March 26, 1971 in Fall River, Massachusetts, United States. American chef who gained fame as an expert judge on Chopped, the Food Network reality series Chopped.

    He played a minor role on the screen in 2005’s Will Smith film Hitch.

    Chris Santos (born March 26, 1971) is a New York City chef and the former executive chef and owner of The Stanton Social in New York’s Lower East Side. In late 2010, he opened Beauty & Essex, a 250-seat, 10,000-square-foot (930 m) restaurant in the heart of the lower east side. He is a recurring guest judge on the Food Network show Chopped, and is developing a show of his own.

    He attended Bristol High School in Bristol, Rhode Island, graduating in 1989. Upon graduation, Santos studied culinary arts at Johnson & Wales University in Providence, Rhode Island, initially planning on attending for only two years. However, after the school awarded him a fellowship, he remained for two more years studying restaurant and hotel management, graduating in 1993 with honors.

    He was raised with an older sister, and two brothers older than him. He married Taryn Santos in 2009.

    In 2000, Santos returned to NYC. One of his first kitchen jobs upon returning to the US was at Boston’s French-inspired Cranebook Restaurant and Tearoom.
