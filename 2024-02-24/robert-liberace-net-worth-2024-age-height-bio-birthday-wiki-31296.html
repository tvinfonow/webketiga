    Robert Liberace Biography

    Robert Liberace is one of the most popular and richest American artist who was born on January 2, 1967 in Pomona, New York, United States. Robert Liberace (born January 2, 1967 in Pomona, New York) is an American realist artist. He attended the George Washington University, from which he received both his bachelor of liberal arts and masters of fine arts degrees. There he was also a recipient of a Morris Louis scholarship. Among his teachers was the painter Frank Wright, who he credits with having instilled into him a love of the old masters.
