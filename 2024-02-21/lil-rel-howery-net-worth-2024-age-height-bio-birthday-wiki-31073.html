American comedian and actor Milton Howery Jr. was born December 17, 1979. He is most well-known for his roles in The Carmichael Show, NBC’s comedy series. He was also the creator and co-producer of Rel.

    Howery was a passenger when a car that had struck another vehicle in University Village, Chicago in June 2016 killed him. Howery and the driver of the other vehicle argued, and he finally called 911. Howery allegedly hit him in the face. Howery was not in the car that Howery was driving. Howery was arrested by police officers on misdemeanor battery charges. Howery was not found guilty of the charge. Howery was sued by the driver for injuries sustained in the fight.
