Ricky Powell, born November 20, 1961, is an American photographer. He is the author or four books. Powell is an environmental portrait photographer. Although Powell was initially famous for his close relationship with the Beastie Boys in his early years, his intimate photos are now widely known. They have appeared in The New York Times and The New York Post, The Daily News, The Village Voice. TIME, Newsweek. VIBE. Rolling Stone.

    Powell initially saw photography as a hobby. He took random pictures of his family and friends. In 1985, Powell was bitten by a girlfriend who “played [Powell] as a soggy cannoli”, he said to The New Yorker. He then took the camera she left at his house and promised that he would be mad for making such a bad decision.

    Released in 1998, Oh Snap!: The Rap Photography of Ricky Powell featured 88 photos, 53 of which were in full color. Powell’s first book, “Oh Snap!” was a ten-year retrospective of some of the best known rap and hip hop artists of all time, including old school rappers Public Enemy, Run DMC and LL Cool J to some of the biggest stars of the day.

    Powell exhibited both his photographs and his signature street style at BRAVE Art, Whistler in 2006.
