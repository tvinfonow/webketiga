    Carol Banawa Biography

    Carol Banawa is one of the most popular and richest World Music Singer who was born on March 4, 1981 in Pasay, Metro Manila, Philippines. She is an award-winning actress and singer, best known for her triple platinum album Carol: Repackaged. This album was the follow-up to her debut album Carol. She was also seen in the films Hiling and Tanging Yaman.

    Both she and Edu Menzano had roles in Tanging Yaman.

    Banawa took a break from the Philippine music scene in August 2003 to attend to the treatment of her father who was exposed to lethal levels of carbon monoxide. She is now married and has two children. She is an OR registered nurse.

    She has performed at the Madison Square Garden in New York at the 2003 New York Music Festival.
