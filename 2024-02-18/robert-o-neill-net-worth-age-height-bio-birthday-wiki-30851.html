    Robert O'Neill Biography

    Robert O'Neill is one of the most popular and richest War Hero who was born on April 10, 1976 in Montana, Montana, United States. Notable for his participation in the 2011 Abbottabad raid that resulted in the killing of Taliban leader and international terrorist Osama bin Laden, this U.S. Navy SEAL claimed to have shot bin Laden in the head during the top secret operation.

    O’Neill was involved in the 2005 military initiative – Operation Red Wings – that saved the life of his fellow Navy SEAL Marcus Luttrell.

    He spent his childhood and teenage years in the copper mining town of Butte, Montana. In August 2017, he married Jessica Halpin.
