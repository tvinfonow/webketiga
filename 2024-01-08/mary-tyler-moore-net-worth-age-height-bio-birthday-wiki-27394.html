    Top Facts about Mary Tyler Moore

  • Mary Tyler Moore was born on December 29, 1936.
  • She began her career as a dancer and model.
  • Her breakthrough role was as Laura Petrie in “The Dick Van Dyke Show.”
  • Moore won seven Emmy Awards throughout her career.
  • She founded MTM Enterprises with then-husband Grant Tinker.
  • The Mary Tyler Moore Show ran for seven seasons from 1970-1977.
  • Moore’s portrayal of an independent working woman was groundbreaking.
  • She received a Lifetime Achievement Award at the 2012 Screen Actors Guild Awards.
  • Moore passed away on January 25, 2017 at age 80 due to complications from pneumonia.
  • Her legacy continues to inspire women in entertainment and beyond.
