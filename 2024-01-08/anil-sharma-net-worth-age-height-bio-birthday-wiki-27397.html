    Anil Sharma is one of the most popular and richest Film Director who was born on June 30, 1956 in Kullu, Himachal Pradesh, India.

    Sharma was also Minister of State for Youth Services, Sports and Forest in Virbhadra Singh Government from 1993 to 1996.
