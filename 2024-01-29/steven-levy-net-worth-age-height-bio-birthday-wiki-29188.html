    Steven Levy Biography

    Steven Levy is one of the most popular and richest Author who was born on January 26, 1951 in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States. Steven Levy (born 1951) is an American journalist, who has written numerous books about technology, computers, cryptography and the internet, cybersecurity and privacy.

    Levy was awarded his “Computer Press Association Award” for a paper he co-wrote in 1998 regarding his work on the Year 2000 problem.

    He published an article titled Hackers The heroes of the Computer Revolution, where he explained an “hacker ethic”, which served as a guideline for understanding the way computers have evolved into the machines we have today and are using. He identified this hacker ethics to be comprised of fundamental principles like the fact that all information is freely available, and that the information that is available should be utilized for the purpose of helping “change life for the better”.
