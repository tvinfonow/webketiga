    Milo Anthony Ventimiglia (/v ɛ n t ɪ ˈ m iː l j ə / , Italian: [ventiˈmiʎʎa] ; born July 8, 1977) is an American actor, director and producer. He has starred as Jack Pearson on the NBC drama This Is Us since 2016.
