    Paul Geary Biography

    Paul Geary is one of the most popular and richest Drummer who was born on July 24, 1961 in Medford, Massachusetts, United States.

    Geary founded Global Artist Management in Boston in 1995. One of his first signings was a then little-known Boston rock band Godsmack who would go on to sell more than fifteen million albums under his watch. Geary ran Global Artist Management for 10 years before being acquired by music mogul Irving Azoff and relocating to Los Angeles in 2005.

    Geary worked alongside Irving Azoff and Jared Paul at “AGP” (for Azoff, Geary, Paul) Management (a division of Front Line Management Group) until January 2013, at which time Irving Azoff resigned from the company, and Geary and his staff returned once more to operate under the moniker “Global Artist Management”.
