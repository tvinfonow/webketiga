    Pamela Brown is one of the most popular and richest Journalist who was born on November 29, 1983 in Lexington, Kentucky, United States. American television reporter and newscaster who is known for working as a Justice Correspondent for CNN. She is also known for being the Senior White House Correspondent for the network and has been featured in articles within The New York Times, The Washington Post, and The Lexington Herald.

    She worked alongside Jake Tapper at CNN and shared a photo with him to her Instagram in September of 2018.

    Brown has an older brother, Lincoln (born 1980) and three half-siblings from her father’s prior marriage. Brown was named after her aunt Pamela Brown, who died in 1970 at the age of 28 together with her husband Rod Anderson and balloonist Malcolm Brighton, in an ill-fated attempt to cross the Atlantic Ocean in a Rozière balloon, Free Life.

    Pamela Ashley Brown (born November 29, 1983) is an American television reporter and newscaster. Brown works as senior White House Correspondent for CNN. Brown occasionally provided the lead-in to “Politico’s Video Playback”—a daily recap of the previous night’s U.S. late-night talk shows.
