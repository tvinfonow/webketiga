    Darnell Robinson (born February 9, 1991) known professionally as Darnell Roy is an American music artist, record executive and producer. He is best known for starring in First Family of Hip Hop as the President of Sugar Hill Records.

    Darnell began recording songs as a child at the Sugarhill studio. In 2006 he was featured on MTV’s My Super Sweet 16 when he was turning just 15 years old. In March 2010, he produced his first record, “Drip,” with Young Joc featuring Lil Wayne. Roy later collaborated with Young Money artists Lil Twist and Lil Chuckee. He went on to participate in the Scream Tour alongside Bow Wow, Omarion and Chris Brown. In 2016, he starred in Bravo’s docu-series First Family of Hip Hop. Following the debut of the show, he released the song, “NASA.” In January 2017, his single “Not So Low Key Ft.
