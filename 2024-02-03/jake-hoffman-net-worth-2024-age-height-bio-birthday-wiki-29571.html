    Jake Hoffman is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actor who was born on March 20, 1981 in Los Angeles, California, United States. Known best for his role as the adult version of Ben Newman in the 2006 Adam Sandler feature Click, Hoffman also appeared with his famous father, Dustin Hoffman, in the HBO series Luck. In addition, he acted in the 2013 Martin Scorsese film The Wolf of Wall Street.

    He co-starred with Devon Aoki in a 2009 indie film titled Rosencrantz and Guildenstern Are Undead.

    Jake Hoffman was born in Los Angeles County, California, the son of actor Dustin Hoffman and Lisa Hoffman (née Gottsegen). He is of Jewish background. In 2003, he graduated from NYU Film School. Since then, he has directed music videos, and written and directed short films, and is most known for his work as an actor. In 2006, he played the adult version of Ben Newman in the comedy fantasy film Click, playing Adam Sandler’s son.

    Hoffman and his father Dustin were cast as grandson and grandfather in the HBO television series Luck (2012). Hoffman played shoe designer Steve Madden in Martin Scorsese’s The Wolf of Wall Street (2013).

    He was raised in Los Angeles, California, as the son of actor Dustin Hoffman and business executive Lisa Gottsegen Hoffman. He grew up with siblings named Rebecca, Alexandra, Jenna, Karina, and Max.
