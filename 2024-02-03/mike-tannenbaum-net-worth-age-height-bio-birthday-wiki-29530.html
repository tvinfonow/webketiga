    Mike Tannenbaum Biography

    Mike Tannenbaum is one of the most popular and richest Athlete who was born on February 14, 1969 in New York, United States. Mike Tannenbaum, a professional American football executive, was born February 14, 1969 in New York City. He was the executive vice president of football operations for the Miami Dolphins. He was previously the general manager of the New York Jets, National Football League.

    The New York Jets hired Tannenbaum on February 19, 1997, as Director of Player Contract Negotiations. Tannenbaum has held many administrative positions for the Jets over the years including Assistant General Manager and Senior Vice President Football Operations.

    Tannenbaum signed a contract extension with the team through 2014; however, following the last game of the 2012 NFL regular season, Jets owner Woody Johnson announced that Tannenbaum would not return for the 2013 season.

    Tannenbaum was hired by the Dolphins to be the Executive Vice President of Football Operations, starting February 1, 2015 after acting as a consultant for the team during the 2014 NFL season. On 12-31-18 he was reassigned from his duties without a defined purpose

    While he was in law school, Tannenbaum worked as an intern at the New Orleans Saints personnel office. In 1996, he became a player personnel assistant.
