    Sommore (born Lori Ann Rambough; May 15, 1966) is an American comedian and actress. Known as the “Diva of Contemporary Comedy,” her comedic style features a biting sarcasm and frank discussions about money, sex, and equality between the sexes. She first gained fame as the first female host of Comic View, a comedy show hosted Black Entertainment Television (BET) as well as one of the female comedians to join The Queens of Comedy tour.

    Sommore has appeared on various shows including HBO’s Def Comedy Jam, Showtime at the Apollo, ComicView, and BET Live from L.A. She has also done guest appearances on television sitcoms The Hughleys and The Parkers, and she has been featured on The Oprah Winfrey Show. She also appeared on Comedy Central Roast of Flavor Flav. In 2008, she appeared as one of the mob members on several episodes of the game show 1 vs. 100.
