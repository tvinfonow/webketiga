    Why did Gabby Hayes leave Hopalong Cassidy?

    From 1935 to 1939, Hayes played the part of Windy Halliday, the humorous “codger” sidekick of Hopalong Cassidy (played by William Boyd). In 1939, Hayes left that role at Paramount Pictures after a dispute over his salary and moved to Republic Pictures.

    How did George Hayes get the nickname Gabby?

    He gained fame as Hopalong Cassidy’s sidekick Windy Halliday in many films between 1936-39. Leaving the Cassidy films in a salary dispute, he was legally precluded from using the “Windy” nickname, and so took on the sobriquet “Gabby”, and was so billed from about 1940.

    Was Gabby Hayes friends with John Wayne?

    The two actors were very close in real life. Wayne’s children thought of Hayes as an uncle and Hayes often described Wayne as the son he always wanted.
