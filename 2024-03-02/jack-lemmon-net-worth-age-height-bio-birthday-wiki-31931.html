    Jack Lemmon Biography

    Jack Lemmon is one of the most popular and richest TV Actor who was born on February 8, 1925 in Newton, North Carolina, United States. American actor and musician best known for starring opposite Walter Matthau in the 1968 comedy The Odd Couple. He earned Academy Awards for his roles in Mister Roberts and Save the Tiger. He also contributed music to the soundtracks of several of his films, notably Some Like It Hot, which also starred Marilyn Monroe.

    Late in his career, he played Shelley Levene in the 1992 drama Glengarry Glen Ross alongside an all-star cast that included Al Pacino, Kevin Spacey and Alec Baldwin.

    He married Cynthia Stone, and the couple had a son in 1954 named Chris. He later married Felicia Farr and the couple had a daughter.
