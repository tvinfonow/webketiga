    Choi Hyunmi Biography

    Choi Hyunmi is one of the most popular and richest Boxer who was born on November 7, 1990 in Pyongyang, North Korea. Choi Hyun-Mi (Korean: 최현미 ; born November 7, 1990) is a South Korean female professional boxer. She is a two-weight world champion, having held the WBA female super-featherweight title since 2014 and previously the WBA female featherweight title from 2008 to 2013.

    On May 10, 2014, Choi won the women’s super featherweight championship of the World Boxing Association by beating Keanpetch Superchamps of Thailand.

    On January 23 and 30, 2010, Choi featured in episodes of the popular Korean variety show Infinity Challenge where she successfully defended her WBA Featherweight Women’s title against Tenku Tsubasa.
