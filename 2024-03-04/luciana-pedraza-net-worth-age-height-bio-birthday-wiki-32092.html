    Luciana Pedraza is one of the most popular and richest Actress who was born on January 5, 1972 in Salta, Argentina. Luciana Pedraza (Spanish pronunciation: [luˈsjana peˈðɾasa] ) (born January 5, 1972) is an Argentine actress and director. She is married to American fellow actor Robert Duvall, and is the granddaughter of Argentine aviation pioneer Susana Ferrari Billinghurst.

    Pedraza wrote and directed the short documentary The Portrait of Billy Joe (2004) and appeared in the 2002 film Assassination Tango, which was written, produced, directed by, and starring her future husband Robert Duvall. She also appeared in another film of his, Wild Horses.
