Jeffrey Eric Stanton (born June 18, 1968) is an American former professional motocross racer. He competed in the AMA Motocross Championships from 1988 to 1999. He is a three-time AMA 250cc Motocross and Supercross national champion], winning the titles in 1989, 1990 and 1992.
