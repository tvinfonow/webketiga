    Top Facts about Harry Shearer

  • Harry Shearer is an American actor, comedian, and musician.
  • He was born on December 23, 1943 in Los Angeles, California.
  • Shearer has appeared in over 100 films and TV shows.
  • He is best known for his work on The Simpsons as the voice of Mr. Burns, Ned Flanders, and others.
  • Shearer has won a Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Voice-Over Performance.
  • He co-created the mockumentary film This Is Spinal Tap.
  • Shearer hosts a weekly radio show called Le Show since 1983.
  • In addition to acting, he is also a skilled bassist and has released several albums.
  • Shearer was awarded a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame in 2019.
  • He is married to singer-songwriter Judith Owen since 1993
