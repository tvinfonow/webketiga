    Cassidy Gifford is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actress who was born on August 2, 1993 in New York City, New York State, United States. Notable for her role as Kara in the 2014 film God’s Not Dead and for her portrayal of Hannah in the 2011 feature Adventures of Serial Buddies, she is also recognized for her appearances in episodes of Blue Bloods and The Suite Life on Deck.
