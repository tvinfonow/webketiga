    Holmes began working for CNN as anchor of the news and as a reporter in October of 2006. Holmes co-anchored CNN Saturday & Sunday Morning alongside Betty Nguyen until March 2010 when Nguyen resigned to CBS. Holmes later anchored the newscast on his own.
