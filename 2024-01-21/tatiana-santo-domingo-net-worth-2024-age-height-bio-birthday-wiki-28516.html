    Tatiana Santo Domingo Biography

    Tatiana Santo Domingo is one of the most popular and richest Fashion Designer who was born on November 24, 1983 in New York, United States. Tatiana Santo Domingo was born on November 24, 1983, at New York and raised in Geneva and Paris. She is part of the Santo Domingo family. The father of her mother Julio Mario Santo Domingo, Jr. (1958-2009) was the son of Julio Mario Santo Domingo (1923-2011) who was listed as the second richest person of Colombia according to Forbes in the year 2011. Her grandfather owned the Santo Domingo Group and the Colombian Bavaria Brewery, which was among the biggest brewery companies across South America. When he died in 2011 He left a sixth of his fortune his estate to Tatiana Santo Domingo.

    In the year 2010, Tatiana Santo Domingo relinquished her US citizenship, as per the Internal Revenue Service.

    On 6 November 2012, Santo Domingo announced to La Voz Libre that she was expecting a baby. She gave birth to a boy, a son named Alexandre Andrea Stefano “Sasha” Casiraghi, on 21 March 2013, in London, England. As his parents were not married at the time of birth, he was not then included in the Monegasque line of succession. However, due to their subsequent marriage their son currently occupies the fifth position in the line of succession to the Monegasque throne.

    In July 2012, Caroline, Princess of Hanover, released a statement announcing that Andrea Casiraghi and Tatiana Santo Domingo became engaged after a seven-year relationship. Prior to their wedding, she had already accompanied Casiraghi at some of most important social events in Monaco, such as the Rose Ball, the enthronement of his uncle Prince Albert II of Monaco, the Monaco Grand Prix and the wedding of Prince Albert and Charlene Wittstock.

    Santo Domingo supports several charity organizations, including her support for the Motrice Foundation, which funds research into cerebral palsy, and that the husband Andrea Casiraghi also supports. In August 2006, she and Andrea Casiraghi visited Manila for a joint venture between the World Association of Children’s Friends and the Virlanie Foundation.
