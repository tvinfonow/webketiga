    Kevin Owens Biography

    Kevin Owens is one of the most popular and richest Basketball Player who was born on June 9, 1980 in Haddonfield, New Jersey, United States. Kevin Owens (born (1980-06-09 ) June 9, 1980) is an American former professional basketball player. In 2009 Owens played with Sigal Prishtina of the Kosovo Super League. Prior to that Owens played for the Wellington Saints in Wellington, New Zealand and the Ulsan Mobis Phoebus in Ulsan, South Korea of the Korean Basketball League. In the 2006-2007 season Owens played for the Cairns Taipans in Cairns, Australia. Before playing in Australia, Owens played three seasons with the Roanoke Dazzle in the NBA Development League, a minor league system for the National Basketball Association.
