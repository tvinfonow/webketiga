    Susan Page Biography

    Susan Page is one of the most popular and richest Journalist who was born on February 12, 1951 in Wichita, Kansas, United States. Susan Page (born February 12 in 1951) is an American journalist as well as biographer. She is the present Washington Bureau Chief for USA Today.

    Her first book came out in the year 2019, a biographie of the former President Barbara Bush titled The Matriarch: Barbara Bush and the Building of the American Dynasty. In addition, in the year 2019 she signed an agreement to write a memoir of House Speaker Nancy Pelosi, tentatively titled Madam Speaker: Nancy Pelosi and the Arc of Power.

    Page has reported on the coverage of six White House administrations and 10 presidential elections. She has also interviewed nine presidents over the years. She was the founder and host of an award-winning series of video newsmakers produced by USA Today, “Capital Download.” She is frequently seen on the cable news networks as an analyst, and frequently hosting guest shows on The Diane Rehm Show, that was a syndicated show by National Public Radio. The first female host to be the music director of The Gridiron Club show and was the chairman of the group, which is the oldest journalistic association in Washington in 2011, in the year 2011. The president was of the White House Correspondents Association in 2000. She also served as the chairman for the Robert F.
