    What does Pez stand for?

    The name PEZ comes from the German word for peppermint, “Pfefferminz” taking the P from the first letter, E from the middle letter and Z from the last letter to form the now iconic brand name PEZ.

    Is Pez still made?

    The candy was originally a breath mint that was developed in Austria. The Pez dispenser was invented in 1948 and Pez was repositioned as a fruit flavored candy. The dispensers are still manufactured in Austria, as well as Slovenia and China, in factories that are owned and operated by Pez.
