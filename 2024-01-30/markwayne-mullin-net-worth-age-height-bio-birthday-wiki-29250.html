    Markwayne Mullin Biography

    Markwayne Mullin is one of the most popular and richest U.S. Representative who was born on July 26, 1977 in Tulsa, Oklahoma, United States.

    Markwayne Mullin (born July 26, 1977) is a Cherokee American politician, businessman, and former professional mixed martial arts fighter who has been the U.S. Representative for Oklahoma’s 2nd congressional district since January 2013. A Republican, he succeeded blue dog Democrat Dan Boren.

    Mullin and his wife, Christie, live in Westville, a few miles from the Arkansas border, and have five children. He is an enrolled member of the Cherokee Nation, and is one of four Native Americans in the 116th Congress. The others are fellow Oklahoma Republican Tom Cole, a Chickasaw, and Democrats Sharice Davids of Kansas, a Ho-Chunk, and Deb Haaland of New Mexico, a Laguna Pueblo.
