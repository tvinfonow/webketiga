    Takahiro Moriuchi (Japanese: 森内 貴寛 , Hepburn: Moriuchi Takahiro, born April 17, 1988, in Tokyo) , known professionally as Taka, is a Japanese musician who is the lead vocalist of the Japanese rock band One Ok Rock.
