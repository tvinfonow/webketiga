    Greg Garrett (born November 6, 1961 in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma) is a writer, professor, speaker, preacher, and musician based in Austin, Texas.

    Garrett became a professor of English at Baylor University in Waco, Texas, in 1989. In 1998 he was named Outstanding Professor by Baylor’s student congress and Outstanding Faculty Member by the university administration. His courses at Baylor include studies of writing, film, literature, and theology. He was the founding director of Baylor’s Art & Soul Festival, a festival celebrating faith and the arts, from 1999 to 2002. In 2013, he was named the Baylor University Centennial Professor, and pursued research leading to his book Entertaining Judgment.
