    Paul Thompson Biography

    Paul Thompson is one of the most popular and richest Musician who was born on May 13, 1951 in Newcastle upon Tyne, Newcastle upon Tyne, United Kingdom. Paul Thompson (born 13 May 1951) is an English drummer, who is best known as drummer for the rock band Roxy Music (from 1971 to 1980 and then from 2001 onwards). He was also the drummer for the Oi! band, Angelic Upstarts and the American alternative rock band Concrete Blonde. He is a member of Andy Mackay’s project with the Metaphors and joined the reformed Lindisfarne in 2013.

    In 2001, he reunited with fellow Roxy Music band members for a tour. He also toured with Ferry, supporting his Frantic album in 2003 and also appeared at the Isle of Wight Festival 2005 on 11 June 2005.. Thompson played live gigs with Andy Mackay The Metaphors and was credited on their C.D “London! Paris! New York! Rome!” in 2009. He joined the Newcastle upon Tyne Lindisfarne in 2013.

    In 1987 Thompson joined the RAF in Squadron 2624 RAuxAF (County of Oxfordshire) attaining the rank of corporal, he left in 1993.
