    JD Martinez Biography

    JD Martinez is one of the most popular and richest Baseball Player who was born on August 21, 1987 in Miami, Florida, United States. MLB outfielder who debuted with the Houston Astros in 2011, then had his breakout season as a member of the Detroit Tigers in 2014. He signed a 5-year deal with the Boston Red Sox in 2018 and helped the team win the 2018 World Series.

    In 2011, he was called up to the majors by the Astros to replace outfielder Hunter Pence, who was traded to the Philadelphia Phillies.

    His parents are named Mayra and Julio, and he was raised with five sisters.
