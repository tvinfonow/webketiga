    Shaun Pruitt is one of the most popular and richest Basketball Player who was born on November 22, 1985 in Chicago, Illinois, United States. Shaun Steven Pruitt (born November 22nd the 22nd of November, 1985) is an American professional basketball player, who is an unrestricted free agent. He played basketball in college with The University of Illinois before going without a draft pick at the time of the year 2008 NBA draft.

    In the season 2004-05, Pruitt played in 21 games for Illinois and averaged 1.4 scores per contest. He also adding 19 Rebounds, 3 steals, and one block to help the Illini reach 37-2 and advance into the NCAA Championship game against the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill where they lost. Pruitt played alongside a variety of prospective NBA players in his first season as Deron Williams as well as Luther Head who were both first round draft selections.

    In the 2006–07 season, Pruitt started all 35 games while averaging 11.4 points and 7.5 rebounds per game in helping the Illini go 23-12 as a junior. He had a career best game against Indiana University with 16 points and 12 rebounds. He subsequently earned third-team All-Big Ten honors from league coaches and media.

    In the 2007–08 season, Pruitt started 33 out of 34 games while averaging 12.6 points and 7.3 rebounds per game and shot a career best 56% from the free-throw line while leading the Illini to a 16-19 finish as a senior. He was a third-team All-Big Ten selection by media and honorable mention All-Big Ten by league coaches. He finished his four-year career with 1,058 points, 697 rebounds, 52 blocks, 49 assists and 42 steals.

    In November of 2003 Pruitt signed the national letter of intent to participate in college in basketball with the University of Illinois.
