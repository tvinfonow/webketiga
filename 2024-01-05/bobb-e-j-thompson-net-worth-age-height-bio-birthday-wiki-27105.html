    Bobb'e J. Thompson Biography

    Bobb'e J. Thompson is one of the most popular and richest Actor who was born on February 28, 1996 in Kansas City, Kansas City, United States. Bobb’e Jacques Thompson (born February 28, 1996) is an American actor, dancer, rapper and comedian. He is known for playing Tracy Jr. on 30 Rock; his role as con artist Stanley on That’s So Raven; playing Ronnie Shields in Role Models; the role of Jimmy Mitchell on the short-lived NBC series The Tracy Morgan Show, which lasted for one season from 2003–2004; his appearance in commercials for the PlayStation Portable as Marcus Rivers; and for being the host of his own television series Bobb’e Says, which aired on the Cartoon Network block CN Real, before choosing to opt out of a second season to do more films. Thompson also had a recurring role on the hit series Tyler Perry’s House of Payne. As a five year old, he performed Bow Wow’s song “Bow Wow (That’s My Name)”, on a show hosted by Steve Harvey. The video of this has been uploaded on YouTube and has over 3 million views. In 2011, he had a supporting role as M.J. Williams on Tyler Perry’s For Better or Worse. He is currently a cast member on MTV’s Wild’N Out.
