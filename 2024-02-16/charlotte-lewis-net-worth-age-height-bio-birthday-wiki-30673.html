    Charlotte Lewis Biography

    Charlotte Lewis is one of the most popular and richest Movie Actress who was born on August 7, 1967 in England, England, United Kingdom. English actress who has starred in films like The Golden Child, Men of War and Embrace of the Vampire. She is also known for her allegations of sexual assault against director Roman Polanski, with whom she worked on the 1986 film Pirates as a teenager.

    She was represented by lawyer Gloria Allred in her case against Roman Polanski.

    Lewis made her film debut as a teenager in the 1986 Roman Polanski film Pirates. She followed it that same year with the female lead in The Golden Child alongside Eddie Murphy. Lewis appeared in the 2003 film Hey DJ.

    Lewis also appeared in a cover-featured pictorial in the July 1993 issue of Playboy magazine.
