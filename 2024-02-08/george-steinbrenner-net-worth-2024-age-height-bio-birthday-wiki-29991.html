    How did the Steinbrenners make their money?

    First generation. Businessman George Steinbrenner owned the American Ship Building Company and served as its chairman, as well as purchasing sports franchises. He led a group that bought the New York Yankees from CBS in 1973 for $10 million ($61,041,979 in current dollar terms).

    Who did George Steinbrenner buy the Yankees from?

    On January 3, 1973, Steinbrenner and minority partner Burke led a group of investors, which included Nederlander, Lester Crown, John DeLorean, Nelson Bunker Hunt and Marvin L. Warner, in purchasing the Yankees from CBS. For years, the selling price was reported to be $10 million.
