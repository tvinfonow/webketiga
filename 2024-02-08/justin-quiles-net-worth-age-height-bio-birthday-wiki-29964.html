    Justin Quiles Biography

    Justin Quiles is one of the most popular and richest Reggaeton Singer who was born on March 29, 1990 in Bridgeport, Connecticut, United States. The pseudonym J. Quiles, he is a Latin Urban independent recording artist who has released hits with the titles “Orgullo” and “Esta Noche.”

    He has collaborated with the music producer Lelo who also collaborated with Wisin and Yandel.

    Quiles signed with the record label Rich Music, based in Miami, in August 2013. His first single with Rich Music was the song “Orgullo”, and a remix featuring the Colombian singer J Balvin, which became a Top 20 hit on the Billboard Latin Rhythm Airplay chart.

    Since August 2013, Quiles has been signed to record label Rich Music. In February 2017, Rich Music and Quiles signed a distribution deal with Warner Music’s Latin division.

    He has a middle name, Rafael. He , his brother, and mother emigrated back in Puerto Rico when he was young. Later, he would settle at Orlando, Florida.
